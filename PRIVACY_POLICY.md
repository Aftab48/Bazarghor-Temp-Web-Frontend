# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [21/11/25]**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Bazarghorr ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy applies specifically to Vendors and Delivery Partners who use our platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services as a Vendor or Delivery Partner (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service as a Vendor or Delivery Partner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Account Information**: First name, last name, email address, mobile phone number
- **Profile Information**: Profile picture, date of birth, gender, city, pin code
- **Business Address Information**: Business/store address including address line 1, address line 2, city, state, pin code, landmark
- **Authentication Information**: OTP verification codes, password (encrypted), device information for login sessions
- **Payment Information**: Payment method details, transaction history, payout information, commission earnings
- **Location Information**: GPS coordinates, location data for delivery purposes (delivery partners), and store locations (vendors)

### 2.2 Vendor-Specific Information

If you register as a vendor, we collect:

- **Store Information**: Store name, store address, store pictures, contact number, email, description, category
- **Business Information**: Store code, working days, delivery radius, subscription plan details, business licenses
- **Product Information**: Product listings, inventory data, pricing information, product images
- **Financial Information**: Commission rates, payout details, transaction history, subscription payment information

### 2.3 Delivery Partner Information

If you register as a delivery partner, we collect:

- **Vehicle Details**: Vehicle type, vehicle number, driver license number, vehicle pictures
- **Location Data**: Real-time location tracking for order delivery
- **Performance Data**: Delivery history, ratings, total deliveries, availability status

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Information**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the Service, search queries
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates, IP address, location-based services data
- **Log Information**: Access times, browser type, pages viewed, IP address, device information

### 2.5 Order and Transaction Information

We collect information related to your business operations:

- **Vendor Order Information**: Orders received, products sold, quantities, prices, order status, order history, customer information (name, phone, delivery address) necessary for order fulfillment
- **Delivery Partner Order Information**: Orders assigned, pickup and delivery locations, delivery status, distance traveled, delivery charges, customer contact information necessary for delivery
- **Payment Information**: Payment status, transaction IDs, payout information, commission details, earnings, refund information
- **Performance Data**: Ratings, reviews, completion rates, response times, performance metrics

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Provision

- To create and manage your vendor or delivery partner account
- To process and manage your business operations (orders, deliveries)
- To connect vendors with customers and delivery partners
- To facilitate communication between customers, vendors, and delivery partners
- To process payments, payouts, and manage subscriptions
- To provide support and respond to your inquiries
- To calculate and process commissions and earnings

### 3.2 Location Services

- **For Vendors**: To determine your store location for delivery radius calculations and customer matching
- **For Delivery Partners**: To track your real-time location for order assignment and delivery tracking
- To calculate delivery distances and charges
- To optimize delivery routes and assignments
- To ensure orders are within serviceable delivery radius

### 3.3 Communication

- To send you order notifications, updates, and delivery assignments
- To send OTP codes for authentication
- To send business-related updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (with your consent)
- To send important service-related announcements
- To communicate via WhatsApp and email regarding your account, orders, and payouts

### 3.4 Business Analytics and Insights

- To provide you with business analytics and performance insights
- To show you relevant order information and delivery assignments
- To customize promotional opportunities and business growth recommendations

### 3.5 Analytics and Improvement

- To analyze usage patterns and improve our Service
- To conduct research and analytics
- To develop new features and services
- To monitor and prevent fraud and abuse

### 3.6 Legal and Security

- To comply with legal obligations
- To enforce our Terms and Conditions
- To protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and third parties
- To detect and prevent fraud, security threats, and illegal activities

We may share your information with your explicit consent or at your direction.

## 5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

- Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
- Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls and authentication mechanisms
- Secure payment processing

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

- Provide our services to you

When we no longer need your information, we will delete or anonymize it in accordance with our data retention policies.
